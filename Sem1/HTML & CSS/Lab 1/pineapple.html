Pineapple

Description

Pineapples are indigeous to South America, grown between Southern Brazil and Paraguay. They have been grown for many centuries. Pineapples were introduced to Europe in in the 17th century where they were seen as a luxury. In nature, pineapples are pollinated by hummingbirds. Today, the Philippines is one of the top exporters of pineapples in the world.
